Little Shrimp and the Sea Dragon

2021
video game

"Little Shrimp and the Sea Dragon" tells the woeful tale of a little shrimp's captivity at the hands of a mysterious seadragon whose hunger continues to grow.

Featuring music by Shayan Sadigh, and made for the Moorpark College Game Design Program 2021 Summer Game Jam, hosted by Johnathan Bair.

Now available here on Itch.io!
