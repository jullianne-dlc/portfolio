Tip of the Iceberg

2019
3D environment

This narrative-driven 3D environment follows the footsteps of a father and his daughter as they traverse the icy trails of Mt. Gala. Scattered throughout the hike are crayon drawings, imprints, and evidence of peril and loss.

Made in Unreal Engine using only primitive geometry.
