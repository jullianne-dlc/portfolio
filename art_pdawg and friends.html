P-Dawg and Friends

2019
charcoal drawing on tinted paper

"Unmei Arcade" is my love letter to night market-type environments. I love the idea of festivals that are bustling with people and energy, light and warmth.

"Unmei" in Japanese means fate, and whenever I meet new people in real life I always think about how insane it is to be at the right place at the right time to intertwine your life with this one specific person. And so Unmei Arcade to me represents not only the comfort and warmth of good company, but also the place where that company goes from stranger to friend.
