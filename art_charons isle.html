Charon's Isle

2021
3D environment

A story-rich 3D environment created alongside artists Joshua Dayrit and Jose Munoz.

Walking through the halls of Gallagher Manor unravels the tragedy of a young girl - Gertrude - and her unfortunate demise at the hands of a wicked stepmother.

Assets created:
Gertrude (girl in casket), angel fountain, knight statue, rocking horse, teddy bear, bunny slippers, violin, cello, mannequin, book/paper illustrations, chandelier, grand staircase, walls, columns, doorframes, floorplans.

Texture work by Joshua Dayrit, remaining models done by Joshua Dayrit and Jose Munoz.
