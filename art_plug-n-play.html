Plug n' Play

2021
vertical slice
video game demo

In "Plug n' Play," you do just that. As a sentient flashdrive called "USB-chan," it's your mission to stick your face into ports and adapters in order to collect gachapon coins and get yourself out of this strange room.

Created by the "Dreamteam"

Programmer: Vini Vella Sarlo Sanchez
Artists: Joshua Dayrit, Jullianne De La Cruz, Jose Munoz
Designer: Shanhao Bian

My specific roles:
Art lead, 3D modeler (humanoid characters and some environment), animator, concept artist, lead texture artist, scene composition, UI, shader creation, menu screen and logo.



To my team, thank you guys for all the fun memories. Making this game with you was a time of my life I'll always miss.
