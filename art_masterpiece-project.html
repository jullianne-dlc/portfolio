Masterpiece Project

2018
video game

Masterpiece Project is the name of the final I got assigned in my 2018 art history class. The assignment was to make any form of presentation - an essay, PowerPoint, video, anything - as long as it taught about an artwork we learned in class. And so, with all this creative freedom at hand I decided to make a game.

In the short pixelated adventure "Masterpiece Project," learn, explore, and iterrogate your way through to finding out what happened to the missing Greek statue the Nike of Samothrace.

In addition to the game, I also made a parody trailer for fun. It's edited to Toby Fox's trailer music for the game Undertale.
