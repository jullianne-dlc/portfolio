When Bella Lost Her Buzz

2021
children's book

Written by Carol Teitelbaum, aka Theadora "Teddi" Bear, and illustrated by me! The almost 30-page book deals with themes of love and self acceptance, as Bella and her friends search far and wide to find Bella's buzz.

If you'd like to purchase a copy, please contact the author Carol at bellasbuzz@yahoo.com!
